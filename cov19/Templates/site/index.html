    Importance and mode of action

    Vaccination saves millions of lives. Its mode of action is to stimulate and prepare the immune system to recognize and fight against the viruses it targets. Thus, in the face of subsequent exposure to the same pathogen, the body is ready to destroy it, which helps prevent disease. Being a public health intervention par excellence, vaccination has demonstrated its relevance in reducing deaths and disabilities caused by several infectious diseases such as diphtheria, tetanus, polio, whooping cough, measles ... COVID-19 virus represents an opportunity to ensure individual and collective / community protection. This vaccine causes specific protection against the SARS-CoV2 virus, responsible for COVID-19 by allowing the immune system to memorize the first contact with the antigen used. Upon subsequent contact with the virus, the speed of recognition and the intensity of the specific immune response will prevent infection or its complications. Getting vaccinated is a way to protect yourself and also to protect those around you. However, collective immunity would only be possible if the majority of people have vaccinated a minimum of 80% of the population on the national territory).

    Side effects

    For the vaccines chosen by Morocco and as for any vaccine, rare undesirable effects, mild to moderate (pain at the injection site, skin reaction, headache, mild fever), were reported in a few people among the participants. clinical trials in Morocco. The interim results of these confirmed that these side effects were transient, comparable to other vaccines of the same type and that no serious side effects were reported.

    03

    Safety / efficiency

    La sécurité, l’efficacité des vaccins adoptés au Maroc pour la campagne de vaccination contre le virus de la COVID-19 ont été confirmées à travers des constats positifs et des essais cliniques menés au Maroc et dans d’autres pays. A cet effet, un comité scientifique de haut niveau assure le suivi de ce processus.

    • How do vaccines work??

      Les vaccins stimulent les réponses immunitaires protectrices du corps humain de sorte que, si une personne est infectée par un agent pathogène, le système immunitaire peut rapidement empêcher l'infection de se propager dans le corps et de provoquer des maladies. De cette manière, les vaccins imitent une infection naturelle mais sans pour autant provoquer la maladie. Vaccines stimulate the protective immune responses of the human body so that if a person is infected with a pathogen, the immune system can quickly prevent the infection from spreading throughout the body and causing disease. In this way, the vaccines mimic a natural infection without causing disease.

    • How do you know if the vaccine against the COVID-19 virus is safe?

      Like other vaccines, vaccines developed to fight the COVID-19 virus must undergo extensive and rigorous testing before they are introduced into a country. Once used, they will be continuously monitored to ensure that they are safe for those who receive them. There are many strong protective mechanisms to ensure the safety of vaccines against the COVID-19 virus. These trials, which involve people in good health or with balanced medical conditions, are specially designed to identify any common side effects or other safety concerns. If a clinical trial shows that a vaccine against the COVID-19 virus is safe and effective, a series of efficacy and safety reviews should be conducted, including regulatory review and approval in the country where the vaccine is made
